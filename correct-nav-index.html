What kinds of events do you organise?

I'm trained as a contemporary art curator. #PROJECTS

... all silent but for the buzzing ... website (2014)

I project managed and commissioned this website for the exhibition '... all silent but for the buzzing ...' - which considered the physical experience of chatter and silence.

My contribution as both curator and digital producer was to consider the impact of mobile technologies and interfaces on our daily experience of chatter and silence.

I conceived of this website as an online archive that reflected the physical spirit of the show. It also contained web apps that allowed the audience to upload images and comments about the show, to participate in the online artwork 'Red Rope Network' by Ryder Ripps, and a sound archive of the audio guide and sound artworks that were a part of the show.

Studio Visit Society (2015)

I started a project whereby I organised a group of artists and designers at the Royal College of Art to collectively visit and document the studio of another artist, designer, or practitioner at the RCA each day of the academic term. The blog I set up containing our findings was meant to be a gateway to collaborations, so that students could have an informal and up-to-date way of seeing what kind of things other students make.

#CODE

Ordinal Challenge

This ruby program will convert a plain number to the ordinal of the number. So for example, if the user enters 2, it will display 2nd, if the user enters 3, it will display 3rd, etc.

Temperature Conversion

This ruby program will prompt the user for a temperature in degrees Celsius and let the user know what the corresponding temperature is in Fahrenheit.
